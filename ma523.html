Introduction

This is a theory course covering topics including vector spaces, linear transformations and matrices, orthogonality, orthogonal transformations with emphasis on rotations and reflections, matrix norms, projectors, least squares, generalized inverses, definite matrices, singular values.

  1. Prerequisite: MA 405

Textbook
